Our Story

Empowering Indian Businesses

We build smart, simple, and scalable billing solutions so you can spend less time on paperwork and more time growing your business.

Our Mission

At Rupnidhi, we believe that running a business is hard enough without having to worry about complex tax calculations, stock discrepancies, and compliance headaches. Our mission is to democratize business software. We provide non-accountant business owners across India with professional, end-to-end GST billing, inventory, and POS tools that are as powerful as enterprise systems, but as easy to use as a smartphone app.

Who We Are

Built by Neoera Infotech

Rupnidhi was born out of a simple observation: existing accounting software was either too complex for daily shop owners (like TallyPrime) or too basic to handle growing inventory needs (like standard mobile apps).

Developed and maintained by Neoera Infotech in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, we set out to build the perfect middle-ground. A robust, cloud-enabled ecosystem that handles heavy wholesale distributions, rapid retail checkout counters, and complex pharmacy batch tracking, all while remaining incredibly intuitive.

Today, we proudly serve retailers, wholesalers, medical stores, and supermarkets across India, ensuring they remain 100% GST compliant without breaking a sweat.

What Drives Us

Simplicity First

You don't need an accounting degree to use Rupnidhi. We design every interface, button, and report to be logical, clean, and immediately understandable for any store clerk.

Uncompromising Security

Your financial data is your business's lifeblood. We utilize bank-grade encryption, secure cloud backups, and strict privacy protocols to ensure your data is always safe.

Customer Success

We don't just sell software; we build partnerships. Our dedicated support team is always ready to help you set up hardware, migrate data, and train your staff.
