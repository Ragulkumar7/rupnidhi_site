FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions? Contact us →

Q1: How is billing software for manufacturing different from regular billing software?+
Manufacturing billing software connects invoicing with production, raw material tracking, batch management, and finished goods inventory — making it a complete customised software solution built for factories and production units.
Q2: Can the software handle GST billing and tax compliance?+
Yes. The software generates GST-compliant invoices with HSN codes, tax breakdowns (CGST, SGST, IGST), and detailed GST reports to simplify return filing.
Q3: Is the software suitable for small and medium manufacturing businesses?+
Absolutely. The system is scalable and works for small workshops, mid-size manufacturing units, and growing factories that need customised software tailored to their workflow.
Q4: Can I customize invoice formats and reports?+
Yes. You can customize invoice templates, add branding, modify fields, and generate reports based on your production and sales requirements.
Q5: Does it include inventory and production tracking features?+
Yes. The software manages raw materials, stock levels, batch tracking, production output, and finished goods — helping you maintain accurate records and reduce losses.
