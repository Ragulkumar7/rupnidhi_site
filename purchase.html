End-to-End Purchase Workflow

Stop Typing. Start Scanning.

Processing incoming goods and vendor invoices used to take hours. With Rupnidhi's AI-driven system, it takes seconds.

AI Invoice Scanning (OCR)

Simply upload or click a photo of your supplier's invoice. Our advanced AI scans the document and automatically extracts line items, quantities, and GST details.

Instant Inventory Updates

Once a purchase invoice is scanned and approved, stock levels for all those items are immediately updated in your warehouse or store inventory. No manual data entry.

Payables Management

Never miss a vendor payment. Track outstanding balances, set payment due date reminders, and manage partial payments to your suppliers easily.

Meaningful Insights

The AI analyzes your purchase history to spot trends. Identify which products are costing you more over time and discover pricing variations among different suppliers.

Purchase Orders (PO)

Create and send professional Purchase Orders directly to your vendors. Convert approved POs into Purchase Invoices instantly when the goods arrive.

GST Purchase Reconciliation

Ensure you get your Input Tax Credit (ITC). The system helps reconcile your purchase invoices with GSTR-2B data so you never lose money on unclaimed tax credits.
