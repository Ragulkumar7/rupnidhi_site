Everything Your Business Needs to Grow

From GST compliance to barcode scanning and real-time analytics. Explore the 12 powerful core features built directly into Rupnidhi.

Start Your Free Trial

1. GST-Compliant Billing

Generate GST-compliant invoices with automatic tax calculations such as CGST, SGST, and IGST. Reduces manual errors and improves billing speed.

2. POS Billing System

A highly optimized Point of Sale (POS) system designed for retail stores and supermarkets. Quickly search products and reduce checkout time.

3. Barcode Billing Support

Speed up billing operations instantly. Staff can scan product barcodes instead of manually entering details, drastically improving accuracy.

4. E-Invoice Ready

Stay ahead of regulations. Generate e-invoices and e-way bills directly from the software to comply with digital invoicing requirements effortlessly.

5. Inventory Management

An integrated system that automatically updates stock with every sale. Track quantities, monitor stock movement, and easily prevent shortages.

6. Cloud Billing Access

Manage your billing and inventory data from absolutely anywhere. Keep your business running while your records remain securely stored in the cloud.

7. Multi-User Access

Create separate accounts for cashiers, managers, and administrators. Use role-based permissions to manage team access and secure sensitive data.

8. Customization Billing

Highly flexible customization. Modify invoice templates, formats, product categories, and add your logo and custom fields for a professional look.

9. Multi-Payment Options

Accept payments in various ways including UPI, QR codes, Cash, Credit/Debit cards, and online transfers to vastly improve customer convenience.

10. Multi-Currency Billing

Ideal for exporters. Generate invoices and manage transactions in different currencies, track foreign payments, and maintain accurate global records.

11. Real-Time Sales Reports

Track daily sales, product performance, profit analysis, and transaction history. Use these actionable insights to make better operational decisions.

12. Business Performance

Generate detailed financial and inventory reports. Identify long-term sales trends and product demand to ensure your business continues to scale.
