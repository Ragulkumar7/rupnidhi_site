Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to Rupnidhi, a product operated by Neoera Infotech ("Company", "we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at contact@rupnidhi.com.

When you visit our website, use our desktop or mobile applications, or use our cloud-based GST billing and inventory services (collectively, the "Services"), you trust us with your personal and business information. We take your privacy very seriously.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and business information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering at the Services, expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, or otherwise contacting us.

Personal & Business Information

  • Account Details: Name, phone number, email address, passwords, and billing addresses.
  • Business Credentials: GSTIN, PAN, business name, store locations, and financial data required to generate compliant invoices.
  • Customer & Vendor Data: Information about your clients and suppliers that you input into the system to generate bills, E-Way bills, and purchase orders.
  • Workforce & Biometric Data: If utilizing the Rupnidhi Payroll module, we may process attendance data, shift schedules, and securely hashed biometric identifiers synced from compatible hardware devices.

Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use or navigate the Services. This information does not reveal your specific identity but may include device and usage information, such as your IP address, browser and device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, and information about how and when you use our Services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our Services for a variety of business purposes described below. We process your personal information for these purposes in reliance on our legitimate business interests, in order to enter into or perform a contract with you, with your consent, and/or for compliance with our legal obligations.

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process: We use the information you allowed us to collect to facilitate account creation and logon securely.
  • To provide the Services: Specifically for generating GST invoices, pushing E-Invoices to government portals via GSPs, calculating taxes, and managing inventory.
  • To send administrative information: We may use your personal information to send you product, service, and new feature information and/or information about changes to our terms, conditions, and policies.
  • To protect our Services: We may use your information as part of our efforts to keep our Services safe and secure (for example, for fraud monitoring and prevention).

3. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

We only share and disclose your information in the following situations:

  • Compliance with Laws: We may disclose your information where we are legally required to do so in order to comply with applicable law, governmental requests, a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process.
  • Government Portals & GSPs: To provide core functionality (such as E-Invoicing and E-Way bills), necessary invoice data is transmitted securely to government-approved GST Suvidha Providers (GSPs) and the GSTN portal.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your data with third-party vendors, service providers, contractors, or agents who perform services for us or on our behalf (e.g., payment gateways, cloud hosting, email delivery).

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. Our cloud architecture utilizes bank-grade encryption (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and AES-256 encryption for data at rest. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We will only keep your personal and business information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law (such as tax, accounting, or other legal requirements). When we have no ongoing legitimate business need to process your personal information, we will either delete or anonymize it.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws, you may have the right to request access to the personal information we collect from you, change that information, or delete it in some circumstances. To request to review, update, or delete your personal information, please submit a request to our support team.
